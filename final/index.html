Welcome!

The Fredericksburg Video Game Tournament organizes game tournaments local to the Fredericksburg area. We have tournaments on almost every weekend, and twice per month during the week. Which game is played at each tournament changes every time! There are multiple tournament formats per game to choose from, most notably when choosing to play on a team, so make sure to bring your friends! We organize at multiple levels, so players of all skill levels are welcome and encouraged to sign up, even if you're not sure about how you may fare!
